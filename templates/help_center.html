Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage, choose your role (Worker or Contractor), and fill in your basic details. The process is quick and completely free!
How can I post a job?
Once logged in as a contractor, go to your dashboard and click "Post Job." You'll need to provide details like title, budget, requirements, and location to attract the best workers.
How do I apply for a job?
As a worker, you can browse "Available Jobs" on your dashboard. Click any job to see full details, and then click "Apply." You can even include a cover letter to stand out.
Can I add team members?
Absolutely! Workers can manage "Team Members" directly from their profile. Adding your team helps contractors see the full scope of your capabilities for larger projects.
How does the rating system work?
After a job is marked as complete, both parties are encouraged to provide a rating and review. This build trust and quality within the WorkWhiz community.
